Roasted Pork Tenderloin

Description

This is great recipe for roasting pork tenderloin. The best part it only uses 1 pan to cook, so it's very easy to make!

Keep in mind you need some time to marinate tenderloin before cooking, so plan ahead! Once you try it once, you'll know it's worth the wait.

Ingredients

  • Pork tenderloin
  • For marinade: soy sauce, vinegar, minced garlic, barbeque sauce, honey (optional)
  • Cooking oil, baking spray

Directions

  1. Marinate tenderloin for 1-2 days in the fridge by coating it in the mix of marinade ingredients listed
  2. Once tenderloin is marinated, take it out and let sit at room temperature for 30 minutes
  3. While pork is warming up, turn the oven to 350 F.
  4. Preheat cast iron pan on medium until hot
  5. Add oil and let it heat for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add tenderloin to the pan - it should start sizzling if oil is hot enough, it's good to use splash guard at this time to prevent splatters. Cook for 5 minutes on each side
  7. Flip tenderloin once more and move the pan into the oven
  8. Cook until ready - thermometer should read 150F, flipping halfway through.
  9. Once ready, take out of the oven and let rest for a few minutes.
