Quick Asparagus

Description

This is a quick and easy asparagus recipe. Should take under 10-15 minutes from start to finish, which is great for a meal on a short notice. All you need is a bunch of asparagus, salt and pepper, mustard, vinegar and olive oil for the dressing.

Ingredients

  • Asparagus bunch
  • Salt and pepper
  • Mustard, vinegar and olive oil for dressing

Directions

  1. Wash asparagus and snap the hard and woody ends off.
  2. Bring a pot of water to boil and add salt to taste.
  3. Add asparagus and boil for 1-2 minutes with lid closed.
  4. Drain asparagus and blanche if needed.
  5. Whisk together the dressing by combining ingredients in a ceramic bowl or cup.
  6. Serve asparagus with dressing!
